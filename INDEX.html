Maha Shivaratri

Maha Shivaratri is a famous Hindu festival celebrated each year in reverence of Lord Shiva, the Hindu god of destruction and regeneration.It is celebrated across India and is a holiday in most states and it is also a public holiday in Mauritius and Nepal.
